The Working Adult Learner Imperative

Enrollment of traditional college age students is forecasted to drop 15% by 2025. As the future of work continues to evolve, more working adults will need upskilling and reskilling to remain competitive. However, this population of 88M+ continues to be underserved by higher education institutions. Moving forward, schools can design programs with the most vulnerable students in mind to educate more of America’s workforce and help make education more equitable. In this series, we dive into how academic institutions can best serve this population.
